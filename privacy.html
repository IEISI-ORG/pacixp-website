Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 April 2026

Pacific Islands Internet Exchange Incorporated ("PACIXP", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard personal and technical information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information in the following ways:

  • Membership Data: Information provided during the application process, including organisation name, ASN, and contact details of technical and administrative staff.
  • Technical Data: Information related to network peering, traffic statistics (aggregated), and connection metadata required for the operation of the IXP.
  • Inquiries: Information provided when you contact us for support or information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Facilitate internet exchange services and peering between members.
  • Communicate regarding technical maintenance, governance, and regional initiatives.
  • Improve the performance and resilience of the regional network.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations as an incorporated society in New Zealand.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared with:

  • Other members (only as necessary for peering coordination, such as PeeringDB updates).
  • Service providers who assist in our technical operations (under strict confidentiality).
  • Legal authorities if required by New Zealand law.

4. Data Sovereignty & Security

As a promoter of digital sovereignty, we take security seriously. We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access any personal information we hold about you and request corrections. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@pacixp.net.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to all active members via their registered contact email.

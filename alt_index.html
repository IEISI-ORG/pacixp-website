Bridging the Digital Divide

The Challenge

Traffic routing among Pacific Island nations is highly inefficient. Neighboring islands often reroute through Australia, Hawaii, or North America. This reliance incurs high latencies (180-250ms) and significant transit costs, burdening ISPs and hampering regional digital transformation.

The Solution

PACIXP is establishing distributed, industry-standard Internet Exchange Points initially targeting Samoa and Fiji. By activating underutilized submarine cable capacity and providing direct peering, we are eliminating unnecessary transit routes and fostering local ecosystems.

For Service Providers

  • Improved Performance: Provide faster and more reliable access, fostering customer loyalty.

For Content Providers

  • Enhanced UX: Lower latency for quicker loading and seamless streaming.
  • Direct Market Access: Effectively cater to local audiences without multiple independent deployments.
